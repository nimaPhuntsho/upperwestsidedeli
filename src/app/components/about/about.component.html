Upper West Side Deli is one of the cafes located at Brisbane Domestic Airport in the Food Collective at Café court in Level 1 (Near Gate 22- Qantas Terminal). It’s one of the SSP Australia’s food chain. It is famous for grab and go foods and drinks including Sandwiches, bagels and drinks including coffee, smoothies and beverages. It was founded in 2009 operating with more than 10 staff members and providing great service and food to their customers.

At UWD, we aim to make your experience a memorable one. Our energy and love for coffee is undeniable from the moment you take your first sip. Along with our excellent coffee, our simple thought-out menu comprises of family favourites which hero local produce
